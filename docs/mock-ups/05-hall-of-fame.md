## Key UI Elements

### Featured Legend Spotlight
- Dramatic framing for current "legendary" achievement
- Personal quote adds humanity to the recognition
- Full stats provide context for the achievement
- Rotating feature (GM selects, or automated based on recency)

### Trophy Cases
- Named awards for specific accomplishments
- Not just "most points" but meaningful contributions
- Includes mentorship and community impact
- Physical trophy metaphor makes recognition tangible

### Quest First Completions Wall
- Creates aspiration for unclaimed quests
- Permanent recognition for pioneers
- Shows guild history and progression
- Empty frames for new quests invite completion

### Milestone Badges
- Tiered achievements (not just top 1)
- Multiple paths to recognition
- Avatar groups show community
- Encourages diverse accomplishment types

### Records Section
- Speed records celebrate efficiency
- Cumulative records honor dedication
- Community records value contribution
- Clear categories prevent single-dimension competition

## Responsive Considerations

### Tablet (768px)
- Featured legend shrinks but stays prominent
- Trophy cases stack 2x2 or scroll horizontally
- Quest wall becomes horizontal scroll
- Milestones panel moves below

### Mobile (375px)
- Featured legend becomes card with swipe for history
- Trophy cases become vertical carousel
- Quest wall becomes compact list
- Records collapse into accordion
- Bottom CTA becomes sticky

## Design Notes

The Hall of Fame serves different purposes than the Leaderboard:

1. **Permanent recognition** â€” Leaderboard ranks change; Hall of Fame achievements are forever
2. **Diverse achievement types** â€” Not just points, but firsts, mentorship, community impact
3. **Story-driven** â€” Quotes and achievement narratives create emotional connection
4. **Aspiration without pressure** â€” Empty frames invite participation, not competition
5. **Guild history** â€” Documents the community's journey and growth over time

### Achievement Categories for GM Configuration

GMs can create Hall of Fame entries for:
- **Quest Firsts** â€” Automatic: first completion of any quest
- **Milestone Badges** â€” Automatic: reaching defined point/quest thresholds
- **Named Trophies** â€” Manual: GM-created awards for exceptional contributions
- **Records** â€” Automatic: tracked superlatives (fastest, most, longest)
- **Featured Legend** â€” Manual: GM spotlight on outstanding member

### Inductee Notification

When inducted, members receive:
- In-app notification with fanfare animation
- Optional: shareable "achievement card" image
- Permanent profile badge for the achievement
