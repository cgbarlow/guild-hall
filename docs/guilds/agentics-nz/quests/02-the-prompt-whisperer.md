---

## Narrative Context

In ages past, commanding powerful forces required complex incantations. Today, a new form of magic has emerged—the ability to commune with large language models. But speaking to these digital oracles requires skill and precision. Master this art, and you'll unlock capabilities that would have seemed like sorcery a decade ago.

---

## Transformation Goal

You will develop confidence in crafting effective prompts, a mental framework for breaking complex requests into clear instructions, and a portfolio of prompts you've created and refined.

---

## Objectives

### Objective 1: Study the Fundamentals
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Points** | 10 |
| **Evidence** | Text |
| **Depends On** | — |

Read Anthropic's prompt engineering guide and one additional resource of your choice. Note 3 key principles you learned.

---

### Objective 2: Analyze the Masters
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Points** | 10 |
| **Evidence** | Text + Link |
| **Depends On** | — |

Find 3 effective prompts online (Reddit, GitHub, blogs). For each, explain what makes it work well.

---

### Objective 3: Craft Your First Spell
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Points** | 15 |
| **Evidence** | Text |
| **Depends On** | Objective 1 |

Write an original prompt for a real task you face. Submit the prompt, the AI's response, and what you'd improve.

---

### Objective 4: The Iteration Trial
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Points** | 15 |
| **Evidence** | Text |
| **Depends On** | Objective 3 |

Take feedback on Objective 3, refine your prompt, and demonstrate measurable improvement in the output.

## Design Notes

This quest builds foundational skills needed for all advanced agentic work. The dependency chain (1 → 3 → 4) ensures progression while allowing parallel work on Objective 2.
